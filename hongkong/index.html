About This Chapter

LunaTechs started here in Hong Kong — a casual meetup at Central Market that grew into a community of 1,000+ tech lunatics. We run signature nights with talks, hands-on workshops, and boba-fueled hangouts that get everyone in the same room helping each other. No gatekeeping, no coding required — just come boba and tech with us.
